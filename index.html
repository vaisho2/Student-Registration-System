Student Registration System

The Student Registration System is a user-friendly web application designed to streamline the process of managing student records. Built to allow students to register their personal details such as name, class, address, and contact number, this system simplifies the process of tracking and updating information. The platform also offers convenient functionalities for editing and deleting student records, ensuring that data remains accurate and up-to-date. With a focus on ease of use and efficiency, this system is perfect for educational institutions looking to manage student data effectively.
